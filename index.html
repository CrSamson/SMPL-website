What SMPL does

Three ways to simplify the work behind the work.

SMPL keeps the offer deliberately focused: find the administrative debt, remove the repetition, and build the smallest durable system that your team can adopt.

01

Automation

Replace repeated handoffs, copy-paste work and manual reporting with workflows that run consistently across your tools.

  • Workflow mapping
  • CRM, form and spreadsheet integrations
  • Approval flows where judgment matters
02

AI

Add AI where it creates leverage: document triage, summarization, retrieval, forecasting and decision support.

  • AI assistants connected to business context
  • Dashboards and operational indicators
  • Responsible handling of sensitive data
03

Tools

Build focused internal applications when off-the-shelf software is too rigid and spreadsheets have become the bottleneck.

  • Member management systems
  • Inventory and field operations apps
  • Budgeting and planning portals

Administrative debt

Most teams do not need more software. They need fewer fragile workarounds.

Before

  • Information lives in disconnected Excel files.
  • Important updates depend on memory and manual follow-ups.
  • Reporting takes hours and still feels uncertain.
  • The pain is obvious, but the first step is unclear.

After SMPL

  • Core workflows are mapped, automated and documented.
  • Teams update data once, from the right place.
  • Managers see reliable operational indicators.
  • Automation supports people instead of disrupting them.

FAQ

Common questions before starting.

What kind of organizations does SMPL work with?

SMEs, NPOs, community organizations and service providers with recurring operational work that is too manual, too fragile or too hard to track.

Do we need a full software project to start?

No. A strong first engagement can be a workflow audit, dashboard, automation prototype or small internal tool.

Can SMPL integrate with our current tools?

Yes. The first step is to map your current tools and decide whether to connect, replace or simplify them.
